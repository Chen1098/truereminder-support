Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create a reminder?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen, enter your reminder details, set the date and time, and save.

Q: How do I set up recurring reminders?

When creating or editing a reminder, tap on "Repeat" to choose from hourly, daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly options.

Q: How do I share reminders with friends?

Open a reminder, tap the share icon, and enter your friend's email address. They'll receive an invitation to view or edit the reminder.
